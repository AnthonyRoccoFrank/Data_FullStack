Let's talk about influenza, aka the flu.
The flu is a virus that typically occurs in the fall and winter month, October to February, which is also known as the flu season. The flu has a myriad of signs and symptoms, and this can vary from person to person flu variation to flu variation. Common signs and symptoms from the flu are:

  • Fever* or feeling feverish/chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue (tiredness)
  • Some people may have vomiting and diarrhea, though this is more common in children than adults
