THE BURDEN


Below is a bar chart representing the several levels of Flu Burden and their overall effect from 2011 to 2021. The three levels of burden are Symptomatic illnesses, Hospitalizations, and Deaths. All values noted are estimates. No data for 2020 was registered as there was a Covid pandemic resulting in quarantine, minimizing any flu data.

Flu Burden data (in millions)

The CDC monitors the US yearly for the flu. They monitor to see the number of people that are symptomatic, whos being admitted to the hospital, and how many are dying. The reason behind this is beind to understand the burden that the flu is placing on the US and the hospitals currently, and to be prepared for the future.
